Contractor Connect

Contractor Connect is a user-friendly digital marketplace that connects homeowners with trusted local service professionals. Built with PHP and CodeIgniter, it brings together reviews, contractor profiles, and job opportunities in one seamless platform. The design prioritizes simplicity and accessibility, making it easy to find, compare, and hire services. It enhances the overall experience for both homeowners and contractors.
